<product_requirements>
The project, Skylio Hub, is a single-page travel affiliate application designed to be a one-stop-shop for travelers.

**Core Requirements:**
*   **Affiliate Integration:** Consolidate multiple travel affiliate partners (e.g., Aviasales, Expedia, Hotels.com) into a unified interface.
*   **Multi-language Support:** The application must be fully functional and translated into Spanish, English, and Hebrew, including Right-to-Left (RTL) support for Hebrew.

**Implemented & In-Progress Features:**
*   **Main Dashboard:** Features three primary partners and groups the rest into collapsible accordions.
*   **News Section:** Displays travel news from NewsAPI, with a Demo Mode using static data for local development.
*   **Content & Guides Section:** A major feature built as a modal () containing multiple guides:
    *   **Must-See Destinations:** A guide to iconic global landmarks.
    *   **Driving Restrictions:** A guide detailing traffic zones (LEZ, ZTL) in major cities, grouped by continent and country.
    *   **Country Guides:** Detailed information on the 35 most touristic countries, with content fully translated in language-specific data files.
    *   **City Guides (In Progress):** Nested within country guides, this section will provide deep dives into major cities, with a focus on detailed public transport information, including airport-to-city transit options, costs, and maps.
*   **Parking Finder:** A standalone utility on the main page that generates a direct Google Maps search link to find reliable parking near a user-specified destination, without requiring a Google Maps API key.
</product_requirements>
